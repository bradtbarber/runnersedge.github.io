Revolutionize the way you run

Our Goal

RunnersEdge aims to revolutionize the way you run. We want to empower athletes and active individuals by allowing them to monitor and improve their running technique quickly and accurately. With an innovative, precise, and simple to use wearable tech device, the wearer is provided real-time tracking of impact forces on their knees, their cadence, energy spent, and distance traveled.
